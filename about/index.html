About Us

Printable activities created for calm, familiarity, and gentle engagement.

Memory Care Pages focuses on simple coloring books that feel approachable for seniors, memory care residents, caregivers, and families looking for quiet, meaningful activities.

What we make

Our printable books are designed around familiar subjects like home life, gardens, animals, and everyday routines. The goal is not complexity. It is comfort, accessibility, and a sense of success.

Each page is built to be easy to print, easy to start, and easy to enjoy without the frustration of tiny details or crowded layouts.

Who it is for

Seniors who enjoy coloring, family caregivers, activity directors, memory care communities, and occupational therapy professionals looking for gentle printable resources.

Design Principles

How the pages are designed

◯

Readable Shapes

Large forms and strong outlines help make each page easier to see and complete.

♥

Familiar Themes

Comforting subjects can support conversation, recognition, and emotional ease.

✓

Printable Simplicity

Everything is built for standard home printing so families and care teams can use pages immediately.

Why It Matters

Small activities can still carry real value.

Coloring can create structure, reduce idle stress, and offer a shared activity that does not demand perfect memory or fast responses. That makes it especially useful in home care and senior living settings where simple wins matter.

If you are exploring resources for a loved one or for a care program, the best next step is to start with a single easy book and observe what subjects feel most familiar and enjoyable.
